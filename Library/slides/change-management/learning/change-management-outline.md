**Why It Matters:**
- Increasing speed of organizational change across all industries
- Growing pressure to adapt to market, technology, and cultural shifts
- Ad hoc change almost always fails вҖ” structure is the differentiator

## Slide 6: Vahs & Weiand's Layer Model

**Four Depth Levels of Change:**

| Level | Type | Description | Effort |
|-------|------|-------------|--------|
| L1 | **Restructuring** | Optimize existing processes & structures | Low |
| L2 | **Reorientation** | Strategic realignment, redesign business fields | Medium |
| L3 | **Revitalisation** | Change competencies & employee behavior | High |
| L4 | **Remodeling** | Change core values & culture | Very High |
